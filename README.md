# Collagio Armani 💖✨

A fun collage maker that arranges your photos into heart-shaped cutouts 💕

## How to use 🌸

1. 📸 **Choose Photos** — Select 2 or more photos from your device
2. 💗 **Adjust Cuteness** — Use the slider to control how cute and decorated the collage looks
3. 💘 **Armanio!** — Tap the button to generate your collage with a random arrangement
4. 🔀 **Shuffle** — Don't like the layout? Tap Armanio again for a completely new arrangement 💫
5. 💾 **Download** — Save your collage as a PNG image

## Features 🎀

- 💖 Hearts automatically fill the frame based on how many photos you upload
- ✨ Cuteness slider with 5 levels — from plain to maximum sparkles and confetti 🎉
- 🎲 Every tap of Armanio gives you a unique layout and image arrangement
- 📱 Works great on mobile phones and tablets
- 🔒 No server needed — all processing happens on your device, your photos never leave your phone 💕
